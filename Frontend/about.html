About WheelieMap

WheelieMap's goal is to provide people with safe routes to pass through, whether they are walking, driving, or etc. We believe you should be able to travel safely from one place to another, and that's where our map comes in. You can also report possibly unsafe routes to keep others informed and the community safe.

Meet the Group

We are a group of second-year students participating in the 2025 OHI/O Hackathon! There are a lot of frustrating moments in life, and safety should be one concern you do not have worry over your whole life.
